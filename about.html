About Me

I'm a freelance journalist focused on music, celebrity culture, and local news stories that matter. Whether I’m covering community events, interviewing artists, or writing long-form features, my aim is to highlight nuance, honesty, and voice.

I’ve contributed to a range of publications and enjoy blending structured reporting with a personal, grounded style. When I’m not writing, you can find me people-watching, listening to vinyl, or getting lost in research rabbit holes.
